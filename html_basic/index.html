What is a portfolio?

A portfolio is a curated collection of work and accomplishments that showcases an individual's skills, experiences, and achievements in a particular field or across multiple disciplines.

What are the benefits of a portfolio?

Portfolio facilitates clear and effective communication of your skills and accomplishments to others.

Creating an Effective Portfolio

Tailor the portfolio to the needs and expectations of your intended audience, present your work in a clear, logical order that tells a compelling story, choose only your best and most relevant work to avoid overwhelming the viewer.

Include descriptions and reflections to give context and insight into your work, keep your portfolio current by adding new work and removing outdated pieces,leverage digital tools and platforms to create an engaging and accessible portfolio.
